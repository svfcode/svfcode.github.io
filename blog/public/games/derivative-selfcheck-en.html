What is f'(4) for f(x) = x²?
Hint

Derivative (x²)' = 2x. Plug in x = 4.

A car drives 60 km/h. s(t) = 60t. What is s'(t) (instantaneous speed)?
Hint

At constant speed, derivative of distance w.r.t. time = speed.
